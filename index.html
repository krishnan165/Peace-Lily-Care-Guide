Peace Lily Care Guide

Soil for Peace Lily

Use a well-draining, high-quality potting mix that retains some moisture but doesn't become soggy.

Watering

Water your Peace Lily thoroughly, allowing the soil to dry slightly between waterings. Avoid overwatering as it can lead to root rot.

Pot Size

Choose a pot that provides some room for the plant to grow but isn't excessively large. A 6-8 inch pot is usually suitable.

Colors

Peace Lilies typically have white flowers, although some variations may have a hint of green or cream in the blooms.
